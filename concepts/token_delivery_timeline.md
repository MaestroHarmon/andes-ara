The Token Delivery Timeline (TDT) represents the series of timestamps when each token is delivered to a user in a text streaming service. It is a crucial concept for defining and measuring the Quality-of-Experience (QoE).

*   **Expected TDT:** The ideal delivery timeline based on expected Time-To-First-Token (TTFT) and Token Delivery Speed (TDS).
*   **Actual TDT:** The actual delivery timeline experienced by the user.

The difference between the Expected TDT and Actual TDT is used to calculate the [QoE metric](./concepts/qoe_metric.md).
